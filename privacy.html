Bintech Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Bintech is designed to be a privacy-respecting tool. We collect minimal information required to provide core functionality:

  • Account Info: Email address and user ID
  • Inventory Data: You may choose to input part names, quantities, locations, and optional part numbers or metadata
  • Device Info: Anonymous diagnostics to improve stability

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to deliver core app features such as inventory tracking, team collaboration, and smart search. We do not sell data or track your location.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except with your consent or when legally required.

Team Access

If you join or create a team in Bintech, your inventory data may be visible to all members of that team. At this time, Bintech does not support granular permission controls — all members of a team can view and edit shared inventory. Please only join teams you trust, and be mindful of the data you contribute.

4. Data Storage

All data is securely stored in Supabase with authenticated access and role-based permissions.

5. Your Rights

You may request data deletion, access, or correction by contacting us at support@bintech.app.

6. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you of major changes.

7. Contact

Email: contact@bintech.app
